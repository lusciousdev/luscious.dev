About

Collaborative Overlays are a tool for live streamers to give some control over their stream to trusted individuals.

Create an overlay, add the view URL to OBS as a Browser Source, and add editors by their Twitch usernames. Anyone on your editors list can add images and text to the overlay and it will appear on your stream live.

Setup Guide

  1. Create an overlay. It's recommended to use the resolution of your OBS capture but not necessary as it can be resized in OBS.
  2. Find the overlay you want to add to your OBS capture. Copy the [view] link.
  3. Add the copied link to your OBS scene as a Browser Source. Set the width and height to the resolution of your overlay.
  4. You can test your overlay by clicking the [edit] link and adding text or an image. It should show up in your OBS scene.
  5. You can add editors by Twitch username from your profile view. Editors can edit any overlay you have created.
